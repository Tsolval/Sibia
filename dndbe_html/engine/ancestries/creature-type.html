Creature Type

Every creature in the game has a special tag in the rules that identifies their Creature Type, and Hybrid creatures have more than one Creature Type. These types do not have rules themselves, but some rules in the game affect creatures of certain types in different ways. For example, the text of the Cure Wounds Spell specifies that it does not work on a creature with the Construct type. A Hybrid creature can be affected by a game effect if it works on any of its creature types.

The following table describes all of the game's Creature Types:

Type Description
Aberration Alien entities with bizarre anatomy and strange abilities.
Beast Non-humanoid creatures that are part of the natural world.
Celestial Creatures from the Upper Planes such as angels and pegasi.
Construct Artifical creatures which are either constructed or animated objects.
Dragon Large, powerful reptilian creatures, often with wings and breath weapons.
Elemental Creatures from the elemental planes, such as the planes of Air, Water, or Fire.
Fey Creatures of magic connected to nature. Often from the Feywild or certain Outer Planes.
Fiend Creatures from the Lower Planes, such as Hell or the Abyss.
Giant Creatures that are similar to another creature type, but much larger.
Humanoid Bipedal creatures with culture, but few natural magical abilities.
Monstrosity Unnatural creatures from a variety of origins, including curses and magical experimentation.
Ooze Gelatinous creatures with no fixed form.
Plant Creatures that are more closely related to plants and fungi than animals.
Undead Creatures that were once alive, but have been reanimated by unnatural forces.
