Ebonholde

A walled city in the North of Astaedan. Ebonholde watches over the trade routes and the mountain pass which is the only route through the Black Mountain Range. It is the home of The Ebon Eyes, one of the most respected fighting forces in the kingdom. The Eyes see regular combat as the forces of Thum Bresh continue to probe Astaedan's defenses.

Central Conflict

*At War with Thum Bresh*

Populace

Ebonholde, being a mountain city, is inhabited mainly by humans, dwarves, halflings, and gnomes.

Government

The Grande Marshal of The Ebon Eyes, Marshal Ebbrek Darkbottom, rules the citizenry as well, though he has a panel of ministers who handle most of the day to day work of running the city. The Ministry of Agriculture

  • Finbul Fernhollow, halfling male
  • controls farming, farmers, and regulates farming supplies, farming instrutments, and farming subsidies

The Ministry of Commerce

  • Glodgir Natsk, human male
  • controls merchant's guild, merchants, and regulates prices and trade

The Ministry of Finance

  • Covri Cennil, human female
  • controls the banks, interest rates, taxes, and controls the distribution of land

The Ministry of War

  • Emdohr Grodonerr, dwarf male
  • controls recruting and ensures the army has the money it needs to stay trained, provisioned, and outfitted
